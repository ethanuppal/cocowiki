Pointy

(Related to Discontinuities, but here it is only discontinuous in the derivative.)

A pointy occurs at a point on a continuous function when the left- and right-sided slopes are different. It is in contrast, but not directly opposite, to a "roundy" (see boring).

Honorary Pointy

The honorary pointy is a moment where the curve acheives 90-degree vertical motion, either upward or downward. Although it does not appear very "pointy", it shares many properties withh the standard pointy.

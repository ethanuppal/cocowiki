Boring

When something is termed "boring" by Mr. Cocoros in a mathematical context, he means not that the concept is unamusing and dry, but rather that the indicated item has something to do with continuity. A "boring" point, for example, refers to a point that is not a pothole, brooklyn pothole, chunnel, or pointy/honorary pointy (see also Discontinuities).

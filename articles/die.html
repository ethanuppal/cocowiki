Die!

"What's in the denominator? So what has to go die?"

Mr. Cocoros calls the canceling out of common factors in the numerator and denominator of a function or on opposite sides of an algebraic function "dying" or "killing." When he strikes out the moribund terms with chalk on the blackboard, he repeats the word "die" emphatically for every slash he makes.

Baron von Limit

Arpita's drawing of Baron von Integral (left) and Baron von Limit (right).

Baron von Limit has been variously described as (1) a Manganese person, (2) a one-sided or two-sided creature, and (3) someone who "comes along," among other things. In the latter case, he often follows the plot element of "Along comes Baron von Limit."

The one-sidedness and two-sidedness is of course the direction(s) from which the limiting value is approached.

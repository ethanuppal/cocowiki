Quotient Rule

Story

A dwarf wants to climb a mountain. They start low and go to d high part. Then, they go down from high to d low part. All over the square cave below.

(low * d high - high d low) / (below)^2

Thus, for x/y,

(y * x' - x * y') / (y)^2

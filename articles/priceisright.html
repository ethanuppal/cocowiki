Price is Right

The Price is Right is a game show wherein contestants are told to "come on down." Mr. Cocoros uses this to describe the logarithmic property where a power inside a logarithm can "come on down" out of the log's guts and appear as a multiplier. For example, log(x^2) = 2 * log(x)

The reverse of this rule is "come on up," where the multiplier becomes a power inside the log's guts.

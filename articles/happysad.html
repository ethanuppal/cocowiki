Happy and Sad

A curve is happy when (1) its derivative is positive or (2) it is shaped like a smile; a curve is sad when (1) its derivative is negative or (2) it is shaped like a frown. Mr. Cocoros may mime happiness or sadness as well as adjust his vocal acoustics to illustrate so.

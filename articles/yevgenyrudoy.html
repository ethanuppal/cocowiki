Yevgeny Rudoy

Yevgeny Rudoy is a former student of Mr. Cocoros. They are often employed by metonomy (as "The Yevgeny Rudoy") to mean the simplest correct answer to a problem. For example, the Yevgeny Rudoy function is a point and the Yevgeny Rudoy curve between two points is a straight line. They tend to be boring; however, Mr. Cocoros would not like to consider the continuity of a point.

Yevgeny Rudoy potentially works for the NSA.
